# Safety: Narrow Access Hazards

## Summary
Trucks are wide and need room to maneuver.

## Key Details
- **Width**: Need at least 10 feet of width.
- **Mirrors**: Stick out further than the tires.
- **Gates**: Must be wide enough.

## When This Applies
- "I have a narrow gate."
- "The driveway is tight."

## How The Agent Should Respond
"Our trucks need about 10 feet of width to get through safely. Will they fit through your gate/driveway?"

## Red Flags / Misconceptions
- Brick pillars at driveway entrances are a common hazard.
