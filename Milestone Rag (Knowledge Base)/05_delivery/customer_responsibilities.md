# Customer Responsibilities

## Summary
What the customer needs to do.

## Key Details
- **Clear Access**: Gates open, cars moved.
- **Marking**: Mark the dump spot (cone, tarp).
- **Presence**: Ideally be home, or mark spot clearly.

## How The Agent Should Respond
"Please make sure the driveway is clear of cars and the spot where you want it dumped is marked with a cone or tarp."
