# #8 Gravel

## Summary
#8 Gravel is a small, clean stone, 3/8″ to 1/2″ wide. It is often washed to remove dust.

## Key Details
- **Size**: 3/8″ to 1/2″ (about dime/fingernail size).
- **Type**: Washed crushed stone.
- **Uses**: Chip Seal, Dog Areas, Water Displays, Walkways.

## When This Applies
- Decorative walkways.
- Dog runs (easy to clean).
- Under decks.

## What The Customer Usually Asks
- "I need small stone for a walkway."
- "What's good for a dog run?"

## How The Agent Should Respond
"#8 gravel is a great choice. It's small, clean, and looks good. It's often used for walkways or dog areas because it drains well and is easy to scoop."

## Red Flags / Misconceptions
- Sometimes confused with Pea Gravel (which is round). #8 is usually crushed (angular).
- Good for drainage.

## Internal Notes (Not customer-facing)
- "3/8-inch washed stone".
