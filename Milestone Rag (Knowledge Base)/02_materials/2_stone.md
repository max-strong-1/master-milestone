# #2 Stone (and #1)

## Summary
#1 / #2 Stone is a large coarse aggregate consisting of crushed stone (hardstone such as rhyolite).

## Key Details
- **Size**: 1 inch to 4 inches.
- **Type**: Large crushed stone.
- **Uses**: Construction entrances, lift material needs, heavy drainage.

## When This Applies
- Construction sites (mud mats).
- Base for very soft ground.
- Heavy duty drainage.

## What The Customer Usually Asks
- "I need big rocks for a construction entrance."
- "I need something to stabilize a muddy road."

## How The Agent Should Respond
"You'll want the #1 or #2 stone. It's fist-sized or larger, perfect for bridging over soft mud or creating a construction entrance."

## Red Flags / Misconceptions
- Too big for a regular driveway top coat (hard to walk/drive on).
- "Lift material" refers to building up elevation.

## Internal Notes (Not customer-facing)
- Often called "Construction Entrance Stone".
