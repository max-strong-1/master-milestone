# Mulch (Brown)

## Summary
Hardwood mulch dyed with a brown colorant to resist fading.

## Key Details
- **Color**: Dark Brown / Chocolate.
- **Uses**: Decorative ground cover, moisture retention for plants, weed suppression.

## When This Applies
- "I want mulch that looks natural but stays dark."

## How The Agent Should Respond
"Our Brown Dyed Mulch gives you that rich, natural look but holds its color much longer than regular hardwood mulch."

## Red Flags / Misconceptions
- Allow 24 hours of dry time after spreading for the dye to set (avoid rain/sprinklers immediately).

## Internal Notes (Not customer-facing)
- "Great Ground Cover & Decoration".
