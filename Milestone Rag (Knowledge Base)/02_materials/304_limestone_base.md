# #304 Limestone Base

## Summary
#304 Gravel is a low-cost, highly compactable material with a balance of fines (dust) and larger stones. It is the standard "base" material.

## Key Details
- **Size**: From 2 inches down to dust.
- **Type**: Base material (contains fines).
- **Compaction**: Highly compactable.

## When This Applies
- Base for driveways.
- Base for parking lots.
- Base for sidewalks/patios.
- Ramps.

## What The Customer Usually Asks
- "I need a base for my driveway."
- "I need something that packs down hard."

## How The Agent Should Respond
"#304 is your heavy-duty base. It has big rocks and dust, so it packs down like concrete. Use this for the foundation of your driveway."

## Red Flags / Misconceptions
- Do not use for drainage (the dust clogs water flow).
- Do not use as a top coat if you want a clean look (it will be dusty and muddy when wet).
